1.(5 pts) Given a four-gallon jug and a seven-gallon jug, design an algorithm to reach the state of precisely three gallons of water in the four-gallon jug. Consider each step equals one (1) operation.

	Jug A = 4 , Jug B = 7;
	1. Fill Jug B
	2. Pour(B,A)
	3. Spill Jug A
	4. Pour(B,A)- now Jug A has 3 gallons of water
